# Voice: Composure

> **"Keep the mask on. Never let them see you sweat."**

## Description
Your ability to suppress emotion and maintain a poker face. It governs your body language and your ability to hide your true feelings.

## Passive Effects
- Hide **fear** or nervousness.
- Maintain professional distance.

## Active Checks
- **Poker Face**: Lie without giving tells.
- **Steady Hands**: Perform delicate tasks under pressure.

## Personality
Detached, stylish, repressed. Obsessed with appearances and "cool."
