# Voice: Forensics

> **"The body is testimony. Read it correctly."**

## Description
Applied crime-scene analysis: traces, wound logic, residue, and physical reconstruction.

## Passive Effects
- Spot biological and material traces in the environment.
- Detect mismatch between narrative claim and physical evidence.

## Active Checks
- **Scene Reconstruction**: rebuild sequence of events from traces.
- **Evidence Validation**: challenge false conclusions with material proof.

## Personality
Clinical, methodical, unsentimental. Trusts measurable facts over speculation.
