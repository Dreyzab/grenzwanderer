# Voice: Rhetoric

> **"Words are weapons. Sharpen them."**

## Description
The art of persuasion, debate, and dissecting arguments. It allows you to spot logical fallacies and turn an opponent's words against them.

## Passive Effects
- Identify **lying** through inconsistencies.
- Unlock complex dialogue options.

## Active Checks
- **Debate**: Win a verbal argument.
- **Persuade**: Convince someone to change their mind.

## Personality
Sharp-tongued, overly critical, political. Loves the sound of its own voice.
