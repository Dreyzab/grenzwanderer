# Voice: Shivers

> **"The city is breathing. Can you hear the rattle in its lungs?"**

## Description
Your connection to the spirit of the city itself. It allows you to "hear" the streets, sense danger in the architecture, and know things you shouldn't.

## Passive Effects
- Sense **distant events** happening in the city.
- Feel the "mood" of a neighborhood.

## Active Checks
- **Listen**: Tune into the city's supernatural frequency.
- **Navigate**: Find your way through the unknown.

## Personality
Poetic, fearful, mystical. Speaks for the inanimate world.
