# Voice: Suggestion

> **"It wasn't a command. It was... a friendly idea."**

## Description
The power of soft influence. Charming, insinuating, and guiding others to do what you want without them realizing it.

## Passive Effects
- Sense **desire** and susceptibility.
- Smooth over social blunders.

## Active Checks
- **Charm**: Win someone over.
- **Implant Idea**: Make someone think an idea was theirs.

## Personality
Seductive, manipulative, slippery. Preferred method is honey rather than vinegar.
