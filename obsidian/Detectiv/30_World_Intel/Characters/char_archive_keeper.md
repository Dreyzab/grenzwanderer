# Archive Keeper

## Dossier

- **Role**: Controls document throughput and procedural bottlenecks in city archives.
- **Age / Appearance**: 40s, wire spectacles, starch-collar dress, dry humor.
- **Archetype**: The Bureaucratic Gate
- **Origin**: University-trained archivist recruited by city administration.

## Psyche Profile (Parliament Perception)

| Voice        | Reaction                                           | Threshold |

| Encyclopedia | "Tests callers with historical precision traps."   | 8         |
| Authority    | "Respects stamped legitimacy over urgency."        | 7         |
| Deception    | "Detects forged document tone before paper flaws." | 8         |

## Secrets

- **Surface**: Maintains deed, permit, and legacy account records.
- **Hidden**: Knows which files were quietly withdrawn pre-robbery.
- **Core**: Only Intrusion route identifies off-ledger index card cache.

## Evolution

- **Stage 1** (start): Strict formal process and delay tactics.
- **Stage 2** (after clue unlock): Grants supervised fast access once pressure and legitimacy align.
- **Stage 3** (finale): Provides definitive ownership trail or closes the archive under Hegenauer's order.
