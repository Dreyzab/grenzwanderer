## Dossier

- **Role**: Lyceum chemistry master whose official death and quiet reinstatement do not align.

- **Archetype**: The Reinstated Technician
- **Origin**: Former imperial sapper, later Freiburg lyceum chemistry master.

- **Surface**: Teaches chemistry and keeps his classroom obsessively ordered.
