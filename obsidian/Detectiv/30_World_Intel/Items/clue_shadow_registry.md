# clue_shadow_registry

## Description

An off-ledger civic register used to track citizens officially declared dead and later re-entered into controlled administrative existence.

- **Scene**: [[10_Narrative/Scenes/node_dead_registry_shadow_ledger|Dead Registry Shadow Ledger]]

- **Flag set**: `clue_shadow_registry`

## Gameplay Impact

- Re-frames earlier contradictions as policy rather than isolated fraud
- Verifies the returned-chemist line and can harden the thermite rumor into a credible bank-side hook
