## Atmosphere (Sensory)

- **Sight**: Blackboard formulas, reagent cabinets, polished desks, scorched sink rims.
- **Sound**: Chalk taps, glass clicks, disciplined recitation.
- **Smell**: Sulfur, coal heat, damp paper, acid rinse.
- **Light**: Tall classroom windows and harsher bench lamps near the practical room.
- **Mood**: Order strained by something too hot to stay scholastic.
