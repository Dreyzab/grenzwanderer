# Сцена: Anna's Tip

"If the dead are waking, you will need more than a press pass." She slides a heavy brass key across the table, hiding it beneath the financial section of a discarded newspaper. It is cold, jagged, and smells faintly of ozone and old iron. "A cast of the archive door. Do not linger there. Some silences should not be broken."
