# Сцена: Cafe Riegler

A silver teaspoon clinks sparsely against fine porcelain. Cafe Riegler smells faintly of almond cake and old, damp wool. Through the window, the Schwabentor looms, indifferent to the gray afternoon. Anna Mahler sits opposite you. She looks tired, though her posture remains impeccably composed, like a heron waiting in the shallows. "The ink in this city is drying up," she says softly, watching the Bächle flow outside. "But I suspect you haven't invited me here to discuss the prose."
