# Сцена: Anna's Secret

Anna’s eyes dart toward the door before returning to her cooling tea. "There is a ledger at the Provincial Bank. It is heavy, bound in black leather," she murmurs, her voice barely carrying over the scrape of chairs. "But it is what is absent that matters. Blank pages where the month of November should be. The silence in those pages... it consumes people."
