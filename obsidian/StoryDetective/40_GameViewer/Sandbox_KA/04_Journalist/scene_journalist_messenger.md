# Сцена: The Messenger

PERCEPTION [Easy: Success] - The brass bell above the cafe door shivers. A boy in a damp coat stands dripping in the entryway. He deposits an envelope on the table. It bears no name, only a crimson wax seal pressed with the heavy, unblinking crest of the Provincial Bank. The seal feels too warm to the touch, like a feverish brow.
