# Сцена: Shivers

SHIVERS [Medium: Success] - It begins as a faint thrumming against the soles of your shoes. The ancient cobblestones of Freiburg are murmuring. An odd, melancholy vibration—like the distant tolling of the Münster bells, but coming from deep within the earth. A secret is gestating in the dark. It feels cold. It feels terribly, beautifully old.
