# Case: Missing Courier (Passport)

## Case Intent

Procedural detective case set right after bureau onboarding. The player reconstructs
the courier route using paper records, witness contradictions, and transport logs.
The case keeps occult themes in background rumors only.

## Core Stakes

- Missing courier carried municipal planning documents.
- Delay threatens city logistics and bureau credibility.
- Courier's choices are influenced by a sick child and medical debt.
- Criminal group seeks route access to city hall archives, not only money.

## Key Investigation Points (5)

1. **Registry Office Desk**  
   Last confirmed handoff and dispatch ledger.
2. **Apothecary Lane**  
   Family-motive thread, medicine receipts, disputed witness memory.
3. **Station Yard and Coach Stand**  
   Transport timeline, forged waybill, procedural jurisdiction risk.
4. **Canal Storehouse Side Door**  
   Interception site and dropped satchel strap fragment.
5. **Clerk Annex (Quiet Audit)**  
   Leak channel inside municipal clerical chain.

## Point Contract Per Location

Each point includes:

- 1 hard clue (document or physical trace),
- 1 disputed testimony (memory conflict, bias, fear),
- 1 procedural risk (warrant, custody chain, jurisdiction, political pressure),
- 1 passive skill check that unlocks extra interpretation or safer branch.

## Dialog Variability Pattern

- Core branch always advances the case.
- Insight branch appears after successful passive checks.
- Pressure branch trades speed for trust and procedural safety.
- Failures provide "useful loss" (tension, trust damage, weaker report) instead of
  dead ends.

## Internal Dialogue Notes

- Add short internal conflict lines in each scene ("speed vs procedure").
- Keep voice grounded in detective realism, not supernatural certainty.
- Use internal lines to reframe evidence quality, not to replace evidence.

## Outcomes

- **Full Success**: Courier rescued, document path reconstructed, bureau submits
  defensible report with named leak channel.
- **Partial Success**: Courier found, papers already moved toward city hall cell;
  next quest starts under time pressure.
- **Compromised Result**: Wrong detention or broken procedure; trust drops and
  next quest starts with weaker institutional access.

## Next-Quest Handoff Trigger

Final report confirms planned city hall intrusion and indicates that archive access
is the actual objective. The file references old holdings connected to the mayor's
family line and a recovered Black Forest grimoire, launching the city hall
infiltration quest.
