# 🗂️ Scene: Agency — Orientation (Detective)

## Context

**Location**: Agency basement corridor.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Lotte Weber (dispatcher).
**Tone**: Grounding. The mission takes shape.

## Script

Weber walks you down a corridor lined with locked doors and numbered pipes. Every surface looks designed to be hosed down. This is a place that expects messes.

"You were an inspector in Freiburg. That is useful. You know how to follow evidence and keep your mouth shut. Less useful: you have seen something you were not supposed to see, and now the wrong people know your name."

She stops at a door marked only with a number.

"The Agency does not rescue detectives out of charity. We rescue them because we need what they have seen. Your case is not over, Inspector. It is about to get much bigger."
