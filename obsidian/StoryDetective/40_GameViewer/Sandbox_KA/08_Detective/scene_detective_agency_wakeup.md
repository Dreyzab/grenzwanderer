# 🛁 Scene: Agency — Immersion Bath Wakeup (Detective)

## Context

**Location**: Agency basement. The immersion tub.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Lotte Weber (dispatcher).
**Tone**: Disorientation resolving into clarity. The new world.

## Script

Steam. A ceramic edge under your fingers. Water so hot it hurts and so still it should not be water.

A woman's voice cuts through the fog: "Inspector. You are late, you are wet, and you are mine now. Sit up before I drag you out by the collar."

Lotte Weber. She does not look like someone who makes empty threats.

Your suit is gone. Your badge is gone. The bank, Victoria, the vault — all of it feels like a story someone told you once, badly.

"Your case file is upstairs. Your old life is not. Get dressed."
