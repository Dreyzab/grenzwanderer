# 🏦 Scene: Bank Crime Scene — Arrival

## Context

**Location**: Bankhaus J.A. Krebs, Freiburg. Early morning.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Victoria Sterling (assistant).
**Tone**: Cold procedural precision. The bank smells of metal polish and something wrong.

## Script

A grey morning. The Bankhaus J.A. Krebs sits behind its iron gates like a patient that refuses to admit it is sick. Police tape marks the service entrance. The regular officers have already trampled through. Now it is your turn.

Victoria Sterling waits by the door, a leather satchel pressed to her side. She is too young to look this composed, and too sharp to be here by accident.

"Inspector Thorne. I was told you would be leading this." She offers no handshake. "Shall we?"
