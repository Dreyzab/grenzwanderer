# 🔍 Scene: Bank — Under Investigation

## Context

**Location**: Inside the Bankhaus vault corridor.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Victoria Sterling (assistant).
**Tone**: Focused, methodical. The crime scene yields more questions than answers.

## Script

The vault corridor stretches ahead, lit by gas lamps that turn everything the colour of old teeth. Victoria has already spread dust patterns across the tiles — she works with the kind of speed that makes you wonder whether she arrived before the police.

A deposit box sits open. No forced entry. The lock mechanism is intact but rotated past its designed range. Someone who knew the system.

Victoria whispers without looking up: "The rotation pattern. That is not brute force. Someone trained the mechanism backwards."
