# 💫 Scene: Vault — Blackout

## Context

**Location**: Bankhaus vault.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player).
**Tone**: Violent disorientation. The world breaks.

## Script

You try to push back against the pressure. Your training screams for an explanation — gas leak, hysteria, trick of the light. But your body is already moving backwards, feet sliding on tiles that feel like they are tilting.

The last thing you see is Victoria's face, illuminated by a light that has no source. Then the floor meets you, and everything folds into black.

When you wake, you will not be in the bank.
