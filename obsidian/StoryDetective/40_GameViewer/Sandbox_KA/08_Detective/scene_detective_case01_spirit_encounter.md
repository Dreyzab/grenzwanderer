# 👁️ Scene: Vault — Spirit Encounter

## Context

**Location**: Deep inside the Bankhaus vault, past the safety deposit corridor.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Victoria Sterling (assistant), unnamed poltergeist entity.
**Tone**: Dread. The rational frame cracks. Something is wrong with the geometry.

## Script

The air drops ten degrees in the space of a breath. Victoria's hand freezes mid-notation. The gas lamps flicker — not a draught; the flames are bending sideways, as if something is pulling the air toward the far wall.

A sound rises from inside the vault: not metal, not wind. A voice that sounds like a ledger being read backwards.

Victoria looks at you. For the first time, she has no answer ready.

The shadows along the corridor begin to pulse. You feel a pressure behind your eyes — not pain exactly, but a knocking. Something looking for a door.
