# 🤝 Scene: Weber's Pitch (Detective)

## Context

**Location**: Agency briefing alcove.
**Characters**: Inspector Thorne (player), Lotte Weber (dispatcher).
**Tone**: Finality. The old badge is gone; the new one is worse.

## Script

Weber drops a thin case file on the table. Inside: your photograph, your Freiburg service record, and a single line of redacted text that you are certain says something about the vault.

"The bank was the beginning, Inspector. Not the end. There are patterns moving through this city that your old department cannot see — and frankly, does not want to. We can. And now, so can you."

She holds out an Agency badge. It is heavier than it should be.

"Welcome to the Grenzwanderer programme. Try not to die on the first day."
