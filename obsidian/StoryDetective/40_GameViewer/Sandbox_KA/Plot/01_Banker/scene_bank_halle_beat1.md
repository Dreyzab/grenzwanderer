# 📖 Beat 1: Marble and Money

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The main hall of the bank is designed to impress and intimidate. High marble arches stretch above rows of brass-barred teller windows. The echo of stamping ink pads and hushed voices fills the air."

"May I help you, sir? If you are here for a withdrawal, you will need the proper authorization forms."
