# 📖 Beat 1: The Melted Steel

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The underground trespass feels colder than the rest of the bank. The massive circular steel door is swung wide open."

**[Nervous Guard]**:
"I swear, sir, I didn't fall asleep! One minute I was walking my rounds, the next... I woke up on the floor. And the door was like that."
He points a trembling finger at the vault mechanism.

**[Narrator]**:
"Where the complex locking cogs should be, there is only a pool of slag. The solid steel has been melted away, suggesting a heat source far beyond a simple torch."
