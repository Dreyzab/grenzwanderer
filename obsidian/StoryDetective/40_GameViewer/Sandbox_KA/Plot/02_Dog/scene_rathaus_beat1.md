# 📖 Beat 1: The Mayor's Crisis

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"City Hall is usually a place of order. Today, it is chaos.
Mayor Schnabel is pacing his office, holding a leash with no dog attached."

**[Mayor Schnabel]**:
"Disaster! Absolute disaster! Bruno is gone!
My dachshund, my pride, my... campaign mascot!
If the opposition finds out I lost him, they'll say I can't even lead a dog, let alone a city!"

**[Mayor Schnabel]**:
"He was last seen chasing a sausage cart near the market.
Please, you must find him! He has very short legs, he can't have gone far!"
