# 📖 Beat 1: Steam and Shadows

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The immense glass-and-iron roof of the Hauptbahnhof traps the hissing steam and acrid smoke of departing locomotives.
Hundreds of travelers rush past, a chaotic mix of wealthy tourists, tired soldiers, and anxious emigrants."

**[Ticket Porter]**:
"Keep moving! Luggage to the left, third-class tickets to the right! Watch your pockets, gentlemen!"
He shouts over the deafening screech of iron wheels on rails.

**[Narrator]**:
"Through the thick vapor, you notice a young boy loitering near the ticket booths, his eyes darting toward exposed watch chains."
