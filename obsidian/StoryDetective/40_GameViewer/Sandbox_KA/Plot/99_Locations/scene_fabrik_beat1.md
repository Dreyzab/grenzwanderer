# 📖 Beat 1: Oil and Sweat

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The heat inside the Stahlwerk hits you like a physical blow. The air is thick with the smell of burning oil and hot metal shavings."

**[Foreman Weber]**:
"Hey! You don't belong on the floor without a hard hat! State your business before you lose a finger in the gears!"
The foreman wipes grease from his face with a filthy rag, yelling over the deafening rhythmic pounding of the drop forge.

**[Narrator]**:
"All around you, soot-covered men operate massive, dangerous machinery. In the shadows near the back exit, two workers are whispering fiercely, breaking apart when they see you looking."
