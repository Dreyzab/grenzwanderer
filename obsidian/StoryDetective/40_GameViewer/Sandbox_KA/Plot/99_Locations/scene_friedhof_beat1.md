# 🪦 Friedhof - Beat 1: The Quiet Earth

### 🎙️ [Voice: Half Light]

> The veil between worlds is notoriously thin here. The cold soil smells rich and hungry. The crows perched on the wrought-iron gates know exactly why you've come.

### 📝 Narrative Description

The sprawling **Hauptfriedhof** is shrouded in a persistent, clinging mist. Overgrown ivy strangles ancient, crumbling tombstones, while newer, stark marble monuments stand rigidly in neat rows. Weeping willow trees drag their branches across the damp grass. The only sounds are the crunch of gravel underfoot and the rhythmic thud of a shovel somewhere deep in the older sections of the graveyard.

### 🎯 Key Interactive Elements

- **A Fresh Grave**: Smelling of turned earth and cheap flowers.
- **The Von Kleist Mausoleum**: A heavy stone family crypt with rusted iron doors.
- **The Tool Shed**: Nestled into the perimeter wall, housing shovels and unlit lanterns.
