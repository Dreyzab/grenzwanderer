# 🎓 Hörsaal - Beat 1: The Echoes of Reason

### 🎙️ [Voice: Encyclopedia]

> The friction of chalk on slate. The rustle of hundreds of notebooks. This room has absorbed centuries of debates, retaining the dusty scent of old paper and intellectual arrogance.

### 📝 Narrative Description

The grand **Hörsaal** of the university is an imposing amphitheater of dark, polished wood and steep, curved rows. Dust motes dance in the slanted rays of sunlight piercing through high, arched windows. Down in the well, a professor gestures emphatically before massive, sliding chalkboards covered in complex equations and frantic scribbles. The air is heavy with quiet concentration, occasionally broken by a cough or the unified scratching of fountain pens.

### 🎯 Key Interactive Elements

- **The Podium**: Covered in scattered lecture notes and an intricate brass mechanism.
- **The Student Gallery**: Where political factions sit in tense, segregated clusters.
- **The Chalkboards**: Heavy with complex, perhaps esoteric, diagrams.
