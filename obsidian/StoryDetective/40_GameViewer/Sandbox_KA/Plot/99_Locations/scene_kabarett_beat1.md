# 🎭 Kabarett - Beat 1: The Glitz and Grime

### 🎙️ [Voice: Perception]

> The air is thick with cheap perfume, expensive cigar smoke, and desperate revelry. Jazz spills from the stage, sharp and jagged, masking the whispers in the dark booths.

### 📝 Narrative Description

The **"Schwarzer Kater"** Kabarett is a kaleidoscope of Weimar excess. Dancers in glittering, barely-there costumes rehearse a manic routine on the main stage, while patrons of all classes—from wealthy industrialists to struggling artists—huddle around small tables. The lighting is low and red, casting long, dramatic shadows. This is a place where secrets are bought and sold, and morality is left at the coat check.

### 🎯 Key Interactive Elements

- **The Stage**: A chaotic mix of jazz musicians and rehearsal arguments.
- **The VIP Booths**: Darkened corners where illicit deals occur.
- **The Coat Check**: A bored attendant who sees everyone coming and going.
