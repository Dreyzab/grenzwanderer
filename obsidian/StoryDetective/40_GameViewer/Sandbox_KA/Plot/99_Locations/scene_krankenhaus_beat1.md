# 🏥 Krankenhaus - Beat 1: The Smell of Carbolic Acid

### 🎙️ [Voice: Chemistry]

> The sharp, stinging scent of carbolic acid aggressively scrubs the air, fighting a losing battle against the underlying metallic tang of blood and the sweet musk of decay.

### 📝 Narrative Description

The glaring white tiles of **St. Josefskrankenhaus** amplify the harsh light of the ceiling lamps. Orderlies in starched aprons rush past with rattling metal carts, their faces grim masks of professional detachment. The waiting area is a tableau of misery: coughing factory workers, weeping mothers, and silent veterans staring at nothing. The rhythmic hissing of a steam sterilizer beats like a slow, mechanical heart from down the corridor.

### 🎯 Key Interactive Elements

- **The Admissions Desk**: Fortified behind wire mesh, guarded by an exhausted nurse.
- **The Corridor to the Wards**: Echoing with quiet groans and hurried footsteps.
- **A Discarded Medical Chart**: Lying forgotten on a bench.
