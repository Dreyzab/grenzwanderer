# 📖 Beat 1: Steam and Cabbage

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The kitchen is small and choked with steam. Laundry hangs from a line strung across the ceiling, dripping onto the floor.
The smell of boiled cabbage is thick and cloying."

**[Exhausted Maid]**:
"Mind your head on the sheets. Can't dry them outside, it's raining soot again."
She stirs a large, dented pot on a small cast-iron stove.

**[Narrator]**:
"A pile of potato peelings sits on the table next to a half-empty bottle of cheap schnapps."
