# 📖 Beat 1: Coffee and Order

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The kitchen is warm and smells strongly of fresh coffee and yeast cake.
A white porcelain stove radiates gentle heat. Everything is orderly, from the spice jars to the embroidered towels."

**[Frau Becker]**:
"Just in time! The cake is almost done. Wipe your feet, please, I just waxed the floor."
She smiles, but her eyes quickly scan your shoes for dirt.

**[Narrator]**:
"She opens the oven door, releasing a cloud of sweet vanilla scent."
