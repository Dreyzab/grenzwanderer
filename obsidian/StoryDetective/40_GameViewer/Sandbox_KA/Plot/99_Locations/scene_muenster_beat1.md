# ⛪ Münster - Beat 1: Whispers in the Stone

### 🎙️ [Voice: Tradition]

> Eight hundred years of guilt, salvation, and blood are baked into these red sandstone walls. The air here demands reverence; it crushes the secular mind under the weight of eternity.

### 📝 Narrative Description

The **Freiburger Münster** is breathtaking in its Gothic gloom. Shafts of pure light pierce the intricate stained glass windows, illuminating motes of dust and swirling incense smoke. The vaulted ceiling stretches impossibly high, making every footstep echo down the cavernous nave. A few scattered worshippers kneel in the pews, their silent prayers swallowed by the vast, chilly expanse of stone.

### 🎯 Key Interactive Elements

- **The Confession Booths**: Dark wooden boxes radiating quiet desperation.
- **The Gargoyles on the Columns**: Grotesque stone figures that seem to watch you.
- **The Altar Area**: Bathed in golden, filtered light from the apse.
