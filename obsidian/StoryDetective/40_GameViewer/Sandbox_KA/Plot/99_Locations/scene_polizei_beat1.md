# 📖 Beat 1: Ink and Iron

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"Revier 4 smells of stale cigar smoke, wet wool, and cheap floor disinfectant.
The relentless clacking of a heavy typewriter echoes from the back office."

**[Desk Sergeant Müller]**:
"State your business quickly. If it's another stolen bicycle, fill out form B-12 and wait on the bench. We're short-staffed today."
He doesn't look up from his mountain of paperwork, his brass buttons straining against his uniform.

**[Narrator]**:
"From a heavy iron door leading down to the holding cells, a muffled voice yells obscenities, followed by the dull thud of a nightstick against bars."
