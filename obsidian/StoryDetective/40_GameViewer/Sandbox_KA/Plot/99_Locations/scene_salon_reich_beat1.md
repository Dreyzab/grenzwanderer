# 📖 Beat 1: Velvet and Dust

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The salon is still and suffocatingly silent. Heavy velvet curtains block out the street noise.
A grand piano sits in the corner, covered by a lace doily."

**[Frau Müller]**:
"Please, sit. Not on the blue armchair, that is for guests of honor."
She gestures to a stiff, uncomfortable wooden chair.

**[Narrator]**:
"A grandfather clock ticks loudly in the silence. A glass cabinet displays fine china that looks like it has never been used."
