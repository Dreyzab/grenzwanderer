# 📖 Beat 1: The Silence of Silk

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"The shop is quiet, save for the rhythmic snip-snip-snip of scissors.
Rolls of velvet and silk stand like sentinels against the walls."

**[Meister Friedrich]**:
"Don't touch the velvet, please. The oils from your fingers will ruin the nap."
He speaks without looking up from the charcoal suit he is pinning.

**[Narrator]**:
"Mannequins surround you, some headless, some wearing half-finished ballgowns.
In the mirror, you see your own reflection, looking somewhat out of place."
