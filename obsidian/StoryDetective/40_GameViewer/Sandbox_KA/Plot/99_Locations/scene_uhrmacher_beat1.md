# 📖 Beat 1: The Symphony of Ticks

## VN Script

**[Narrator]**:
"Tick-tock. Tick-tock. A hundred different rhythms overlap in this dusty room.
Light streams through the window, illuminating dancing dust motes."

**[Meister Ewald]**:
"Just a moment... this gear... is... recalcitrant."
The old man doesn't look up. He is hunched over a workbench, a jeweler's loupe in his right eye.

**[Narrator]**:
"He adjusts a microscopic screw. Suddenly, a cuckoo clock on the wall bursts open,
but instead of a bird, a small wooden skeleton pops out."

**[Meister Ewald]**:
"Ah! Perfect timing. Memento Mori, eh?"
